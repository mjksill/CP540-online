Zettelkasten method

  • created by Niklas Luhmann
  • Zettel = paper slip, Kasten = cabinet
  • do not use categories: use tags and links
  • external thinking system / second brain

It was developed by Niklas Luhmann, a social scientist that published over 600 research articles and 50 books in his lifetime.

It is tool for thinking and writing, as well as knowledge management. The Zettelkasten method is one system to store and organize information. You might also think of it as a communication system with your future self. Some also refer to it as a second brain.
